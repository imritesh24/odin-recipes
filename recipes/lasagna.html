Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering pasta sheets with meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel or ricotta. It's baked until golden and bubbly, offering a rich and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (boiled or oven-ready)
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce; season to taste.
  2. Prepare béchamel sauce or mix ricotta with herbs.
  3. Boil noodles if not using oven-ready ones
  4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta/béchamel, and mozzarella
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes
