Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) served with butter-toasted buns (pav). It’s flavorful, hearty, and loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Onions, tomatoes, and green capsicum
  • Boiled potatoes, peas, and cauliflower
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric, salt
  • Fresh coriander and lemon
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and capsicum; cook until soft.
  3. Mix in the boiled vegetables and mash them well.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala, chili powder, turmeric, and salt; mix and mash.
  5. Add water to adjust consistency and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Toast pav with butter on a pan until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and lemon.
