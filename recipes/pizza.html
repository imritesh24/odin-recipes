Pizza

Pizza is a globally loved Italian dish made with a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is baked until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables like onions, capsicum, olives, mushrooms (optional)
  • Pepperoni or other meats (optional)
  • Oregano, chili flakes, and basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella and your favorite toppings.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano or basil.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese has melted.
  7. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
